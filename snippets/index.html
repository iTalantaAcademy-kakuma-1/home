OUR PROJECT

LIFT ME UP FOUNDATION CBO was established in December 2021
our office is locate in Kakuma 1 near Hong Kong market. The mission
of our CBO is to sharpen and strengthen socio - economic empowerment
of the most vulnerable refugee families. The major activities to support come in two
sets,1) providing our beneficiaries with entrepreneurial skills to enable them
to establish their own small business to generate income and 2) to provide them
with technical skills that will enable them to be marketing their goods.
